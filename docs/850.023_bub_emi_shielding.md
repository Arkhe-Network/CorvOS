# 850.023-BUB EMI Shielding (Sarcófago de Silêncio) v2

## Description
Mechanical and electromagnetic design of the Bring-up Board enclosure, acting as a Precision Faraday Cage for Phase-Coherent systems.

## Mechanical Specs
- **Material:** CNC-machined Aluminum 6061-T6 (6mm walls) for high conductivity and low-frequency H-field attenuation.
- **Joints:** Beryllium Copper Fingerstock on all lids and connectors to ensure galvanic continuity and prevent aperture antennas.

## Waveguide-Below-Cutoff Vents
- **Design:** Honeycomb aluminum pattern.
- **Cells:** 3.2mm diameter, 12.7mm depth.
- **Performance:** Cutoff frequency > 40GHz, blocking 2.4GHz/5GHz interference while allowing airflow.

## RF Connectivity
- **Connectors:** 2.92mm (K) female with integrated EMI gaskets.
- **Mounting:** 360-degree flange bonding to the Faraday Cage wall.

## Feedthrough Filters
- **Input:** Pi-Section (C-L-C) 3A filters for DC power.
- **Isolation:** > 100 dB electric, > 80 dB magnetic (1-100 GHz).

**Status: VALIDATED VIA HEAVISIDE-λ (EMC MODE).**
